Calculator Web App

Key Features:


  • Basic mathematical operations: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Responsive design: The calculator adapts to different screen sizes, ensuring a seamless experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
  • Clear button: Allows users to easily clear the input field and start a new calculation.
  • Error handling: The app handles common errors such as division by zero and invalid inputs, providing appropriate error messages to the user.

Whether users need to perform quick calculations or require a handy tool for everyday math tasks, the Calculator Web App provides a user-friendly and efficient solution.

Project 2

Portfolio Website

My Portfolio Website is a showcase of my skills, experiences, and projects as a web developer. It serves as a digital representation of my professional journey, providing visitors with insights into my expertise and capabilities.

Key Features:


  • Home: The homepage welcomes visitors with an engaging introduction, highlighting my unique selling points and areas of specialization.
  • About: The about section provides a comprehensive overview of my background, skills, and passion for web development. It showcases my commitment to delivering high-quality solutions and my continuous drive for growth and learning.
  • Portfolio: The portfolio section displays a curated collection of my notable projects. Each project includes a brief description, key features, and relevant technologies used. This section highlights my ability to create visually appealing and functional web solutions.
  • Contact: The contact section offers visitors a convenient way to get in touch with me. It includes my contact information and a contact form to inquire about collaborations, projects, or any inquiries.
  • My Portfolio Website is built using modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It follows responsive design principles, ensuring a seamless experience across various devices and screen sizes.
With its clean and professional design, my Portfolio Website not only showcases my skills but also reflects my personal style and attention to detail. It aims to leave a lasting impression on potential clients, employers, and collaborators by highlighting my expertise and providing a platform for meaningful connections.
